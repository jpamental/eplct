Bridgham Farm
Conservation Area

Rumford, Rhode Island

Managed by the East Providence Land Conservation Trust

Welcome to Bridgham Farm, an 18+ acre meadow with walking trails adjacent to Turner Reservoir. You can learn a bit more about us, or make a secure one-time or recurring donation to help support EPLCT and Bridgham Farm.

Located next to Pleasant and Miller Streets in Rumford, RI. Limited parking is available at the entrance on Pleasant Street.
