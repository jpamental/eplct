Rumford, Rhode Island

Managed by the Bridgham Farm Land Trust

Welcome to Bridgham Farm, an 18+ acre meadow with walking trails adjacent to Turner Reservoir. You can learn a bit more about us, or make a secure one-time or recurring donation to help support BFLT and Bridgham Farm.

Located next to Pleasant and Miller Streets in Rumford, RI. Limited parking is available at the entrance on Pleasant Street.
